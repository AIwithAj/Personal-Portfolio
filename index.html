Human & Emotion Project

CNN

In this project journey, I began by ingesting a dataset from MongoDB, which I had previously stored. My initial step was training a custom model, achieving a 46% accuracy rate. However, I tried to enhance this performance by leveraging pretrained models such as Efficient Net B4, VGGNet 16, and ResNet 30, with fine-tuning techniques but accuracy same as my custom model. Despite these efforts, MobileNetV2 exhibited underwhelming performance, stabilizing at 24% accuracy within the first 10 epochs. Yet, ResNet50 emerged as the standout performer, showcasing significant improvement. Throughout this end-to-end project, I integrated MLOps concepts, including experiment tracking with Weight and Biases, model versioning, data versioning, and a commitment to modular coding practices.
